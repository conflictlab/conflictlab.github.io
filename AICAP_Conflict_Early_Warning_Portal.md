AICAP Conflict Early Warning Portal (CEWP)

Overview:
CEWP supports journalists, NGOs, and humanitarian planners with structured, open-access forecasts of global conflict risk. With embeddable maps, downloadable data, and alert tools, CEWP enables proactive communication, coordination, and crisis response.

Use Cases:
- Plan emergency response based on predictive conflict models
- Support journalism with forecast-informed visual narratives
- Power advocacy campaigns with early warning insights

Key Features:
- Regional dashboards and live forecast feeds
- Embedded map visualizations
- Public/freemium access with user-level tools
- Exportable charts and country briefs

Audience:
- Newsrooms, humanitarian NGOs, international advocacy groups
