AICAP Portfolio Risk Intelligence (PRI)

Overview:
AICAP PRI enables asset managers and quant teams to integrate predictive geopolitical risk into portfolio construction and monitoring. Using machine learning-driven forecasts and firm-level exposure mapping, PRI helps reduce drawdowns, optimize Sharpe ratios, and flag geopolitical hotspots before markets react.

Use Cases:
- Rebalance portfolios to reduce exposure to regions with rising conflict or protest risk
- Incorporate AICAP scores into quantitative models for Sharpe-optimized returns
- Backtest risk-adjusted returns using historical AICAP forecast accuracy
- Monitor geopolitical risk concentration across sovereign and corporate holdings

Key Features:
- Asset-level conflict exposure scores
- Monthly forecast updates with API access
- Portfolio screening, alerts, and dashboard visualizations
- Integration-ready with Excel, Python, or portfolio management platforms

Audience:
- Institutional investors, hedge funds, ESG fund managers, quant researchers
