## Products/Services (from services.json - referenced in technology page)

### Core Capabilities
1. **Conflict & Protest Forecasting**: Predictive modeling of violence, unrest, and instability at subnational levels using RNNs, LSTMs, and temporal clustering.
2. **Exposure-Based Risk Modeling**: Converts regional instability forecasts into financial or operational risk estimates for specific firms, supply chains, or portfolios.  
3. **Geopolitical ESG Analytics**: Aligns forecasts with EU Taxonomy and SFDR criteria for sustainable investment screening.
4. **API + Dashboard Delivery**: Forecasts accessible via JSON API, CSV downloads, and a web-based dashboard for analysts and executives.

### Products
1. **Portfolio Risk Intelligence (PRI)**: Enables asset managers and quant teams to integrate predictive geopolitical risk into portfolio construction and monitoring.
2. **Catastrophic Civil Risk Forecast (CCRF)**: Empowers insurers and reinsurers to anticipate, price, and manage risk from civil unrest, including SRCC.
3. **GeoESG Compliance Engine (GCE)**: Enhances ESG screening and sustainable finance compliance by flagging corporate and sovereign exposure to conflict-prone geographies.
4. **Conflict Early Warning Portal (CEWP)**: Supports journalists, NGOs, and humanitarian planners with structured, open-access forecasts of global conflict risk.
5. **Conflict-Sensitive Equity Screening**: Screens public companies for exposure to conflict-affected countries using forecast-adjusted scoring.
