AICAP Conflict-Sensitive Equity Screening

Overview:
A targeted add-on to AICAP PRI, this tool screens public companies for exposure to conflict-affected countries using forecast-adjusted scoring. Particularly relevant for sectors like mining, telecom, energy, and agriculture.

Use Cases:
- Detect firms at risk due to operations in unstable countries (e.g., DRC, Nigeria)
- Monitor stock price volatility after forecasted conflict events
- Support analyst briefings and risk-adjusted target price revisions

Key Features:
- Firm-country conflict exposure database
- Forecast overlays on equity performance
- Custom filters by region, sector, or exposure type
- PDF/CSV downloads and automated alerts

Audience:
- Equity analysts, ESG screeners, risk consultants
