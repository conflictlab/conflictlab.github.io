\section*{The Problem: The Need for Conflict Forecasting}

Global conflicts contribute to humanitarian crises, economic instability, and displacement. Policymakers, humanitarian organizations, and military strategists require early-warning tools. Current limitations in conflict forecasting include the lack of real-time updates, granular predictions, and difficulty in anticipating rapid shifts in conflict intensity.

 
%\noindent\makebox[\linewidth]{\rule{\paperwidth}{0.4pt}}

\section*{What We Forecast}
\begin{itemize}
\item Conflict Fatalities
\item Protests
\item Rocket launches
\item Migration
\end{itemize}

\section*{How? Data-Driven Conflict Forecasting}

We use advanced machine learning algorithms trained on historical conflict data, integrating economic, geopolitical, and demographic indicators to provide accurate early warnings. We use stock market, satellite, news and event data.
