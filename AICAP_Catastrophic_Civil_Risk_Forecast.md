AICAP Catastrophic Civil Risk Forecast (CCRF)

Overview:
CCRF empowers insurers and reinsurers to anticipate, price, and manage risk from civil unrest, including SRCC (strikes, riots, civil commotion). Based on AICAP's real-time protest and conflict forecasts, CCRF can be integrated into underwriting workflows and loss modeling tools.

Use Cases:
- Improve pricing accuracy for SRCC cover in high-risk regions
- Simulate correlated risk in multi-country reinsurance portfolios
- Support treaty negotiation and loss mitigation strategy

Key Features:
- Regional SRCC risk forecasts (1-12 month horizon)
- Dashboard and batch data delivery
- Integration-ready with Touchstone/Oasis
- Historical validation and confidence metrics

Audience:
- Political risk underwriters, reinsurance analysts, catastrophe modelers
