AICAP GeoESG Compliance Engine (GCE)

Overview:
GCE enhances ESG screening and sustainable finance compliance by flagging corporate and sovereign exposure to conflict-prone geographies. It provides conflict-adjusted ESG overlays aligned with SFDR and EU Taxonomy standards.

Use Cases:
- Filter portfolios for firms operating in or sourcing from unstable regions
- Score sovereign issuers for conflict or repression indicators
- Document conflict exposure in ESG reporting frameworks

Key Features:
- Conflict-aligned ESG scoring per issuer
- Geographic filters and portfolio scorecards
- Reporting modules for regulators or clients
- Downloadable datasets, PDF reports, and API access

Audience:
- ESG analysts, fund managers, compliance officers, data vendors
